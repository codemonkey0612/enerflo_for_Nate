Enerflo is Purpose-Built

We’re on a mission to help democratize solar energy and spread it across the globe, one well-executed deal at a time.

Enerflo’s Origin

Once upon a time in 2019, a solar guy and a tech guy came together to simplify the solar sales process through technology. In the early days, Pat would code at night and Spencer would knock doors the next day to test the technology on real customers. With Spencer’s extensive sales and solar background and Pat’s experience of building software companies, things quickly progressed.

Now, a few years later, Enerflo has grown it’s professional team and customer base fast, already processing $28B in solar transactions to date. Enerflo’s rapid growth is a testament to the Founders’ clear vision for creating a truly open platform that can intelligently integrate all of the great tools and applications that dealers and installers are already using.

Enerflo’s on a mission: Make solar affordable for, and available to, every human on the planet. It’s a simple but lofty goal that drives Enerflo’s employees every day. We invite you to join us and grow with Enerflo.

Why Enerflo?

“Enerflo” stands for “Energy-flow”... or basically the energy consumption or production of a home. Our homegrown, purpose-built tech is designed by industry experts for industry professionals, fueling the growth of the solar industry in the US and beyond. With only 3% residential solar penetration, there’s a lot of work to be done.
