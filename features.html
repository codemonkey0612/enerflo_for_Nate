Solar Platform Benefits

You may be asking yourself “How is Enerflo different?” At Enerflo, we pride ourselves on being different by design. We’re here to help you do what you do more efficiently so you can focus on growing your business.

Open Platform

Enerflo is the first truly open platform in the industry and we have the most app integrations to prove it. We love to work and play well with others!

Industry-Leading Technology

Enerflo is deeply invested in development and innovation to continue to bring our partners new, leading edge solutions to help them scale their business.

Flexibility

Enerflo is flexible enough to configure around our partners’ existing 2, 3 or however-many-step processes, so it feels like the platform was purpose built just for them.

The Most Integrations

Our integrations set us apart from other solar platforms. Enerflo can be used out of the box or seamlessly integrated with the tools our partners already rely on. What’s best for our partners is best for us!
