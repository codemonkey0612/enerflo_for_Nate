Enerflo Product Announcements - July 2025

By Enerflo Marketing | July 24, 2025

Enerflo’s July 2025 product updates come at a pivotal time for the solar industry, following the recent passage of the One Big Beautiful Bill (OBBB – H.R. 1). This month’s enhancements span Solar Design, Solar Finance, Platform, and Install Tracker—equipping solar companies with even greater control, visibility, and efficiency across the full project lifecycle. New integrations with Greentech Renewables and OneEthos Powered by Climate First Bank expand partner capabilities, while AI-powered tools continue to drive down soft costs and support higher deal volume. These updates reflect Enerflo’s ongoing commitment to helping solar professionals adapt quickly, deliver value to homeowners, and thrive in a changing policy and market landscape.
